Step-by-Step Guide to Becoming a Successful Hijab Seller

  1. Love to shopping local hijab brand
  2. Learn about fashion, fabric types, and suitable hijab styles
  3. Create a business plan and choose your brand name
  4. Start a small online business (Instagram, Shopee, TikTok Shop)
  5. Promote your products using social media marketing
  6. Join local bazaars or pop-up stalls to grow your customer base
  7. Expand your business with a physical store or a website
